# ThemeForest

## Purpose

The original website depended on commercial themes and plugins obtained through ThemeForest. Recovering access to the original ThemeForest account enabled legitimate access to licensed resources required during reconstruction.

## Recovery Activities

- Recovered access to the original ThemeForest account.
- Verified purchased themes.
- Downloaded the latest compatible versions where appropriate.
- Restored required theme assets.
- Reinstalled licensed components during the recovery process.

## Importance

Access to the original licensed resources simplified theme restoration and ensured that supported versions of the required assets were available during reconstruction.

## Outcome

Recovered ThemeForest resources contributed to the successful restoration of the website while maintaining the original design and functionality.
